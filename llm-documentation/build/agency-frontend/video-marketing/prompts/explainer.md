# Prompt — Explainer Video (Présentation Marin)

## Cible

Vidéo de 60-75s pour la landing page principale de Marin, présentant le service aux grossistes.

## Story

```
Scene 1 (0-5s):   Hook — "You're a wholesaler and you can't find enough B2B clients?"
Scene 2 (5-20s):  Problem — "Cold calling is a numbers game. You dial, you chase, you waste time."
Scene 3 (20-35s): Solution — "Marin generates qualified leads for you. Every day. Automatically."
Scene 4 (35-50s): How it works — "Scraping → Clean → Campaign → Call → Close. All in one pipeline."
Scene 5 (50-60s): Social proof — "10 sales guaranteed in 90 days. Or we continue until you get them."
Scene 6 (60-70s): CTA — "Ready to stop chasing and start closing?"
```

## Prompt de démarrage

```
Create a 6-scene explainer video for a B2B agency called Marin.
Duration: 70 seconds at 30fps.

ART DIRECTION:
- Colors: #49C5B6 (accent), #015048 (dark accent), #FAFAFA (bg), #000000 (text)
- Font: Inter
- Style: Clean cut-out illustrations, Vox-like motion graphics
- Background: Light with subtle warm gradient

SCENE 1 (0-5s, frames 0-150):
Hook scene. Show a frustrated wholesaler character (cut-out) at a desk
with a phone. Red X marks appear over "cold calls". Text fades in:
"You're still chasing clients?"

Animation: Character springs in from left, phone springs up, X marks
appear one by one with spring stagger. Text fades in at frame 90.

SCENE 2 (5-20s, frames 150-450):
Problem scene. Show a timeline graphic of the traditional sales process
— too many steps, too slow. A progress bar crawls at 10%. Overlay text:
"Traditional sales is a bottleneck."

Animation: Timeline draws from left to right. Progress bar animates
to 10% with a slow spring. Text slides in from top.

SCENE 3 (20-35s, frames 450-750):
Solution scene. Marin logo springs up center screen. Around it,
icons appear in a circle: a map pin (scraping), a shield (cleaning),
a mail icon (campaign), a phone (call), a checkmark (close).
Text: "Marin does the heavy lifting."

Animation: Logo springs in with scale. Icons orbit in one by one
with staggered delays. Each icon has a brief glow on entrance.

SCENE 4 (35-50s, frames 750-1050):
Pipeline scene. Horizontal flow diagram: [Scrape] → [Clean] → [Campaign]
→ [Call] → [Close]. Each box lights up sequentially with a pulse.
Numbers appear below: "250 leads/day", "95% valid", "2.5% reply rate".
Text: "Full pipeline automation."

Animation: Flow boxes draw in from left to right. Each box pulses
on entry. Numbers count up using spring physics.

SCENE 5 (50-60s, frames 1050-1350):
Social proof scene. Large number animates from 0 to 10: "10 sales guaranteed".
Below it: "90 days. Or your money back." A shield icon sits next to the text.
Text: "Performance guarantee."

Animation: Number counts up from 0 to 10 with bounce at the end.
Shield icon fades in with a slight rotation.

SCENE 6 (60-70s, frames 1350-1500):
CTA scene. A clean card in the center with the Marin logo at top.
CTA button: "Book a call" with a pulsing glow.
Tagline: "Your growth partner."

Animation: Card springs in from center with scale 0→1.
CTA button pulses (subtle scale oscillation).
Logo fades in first.
```
