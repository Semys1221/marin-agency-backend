# Prompt — Funnel Step Video

## Cible

Vidéo courte (15-30s) pour une étape spécifique du tunnel de vente Marin (22 étapes agency ou 11 étapes e-commerce).

## Usage

- Embed sur la page de l'étape dans le funnel
- Social media teaser pour chaque étape
- Explication rapide pour le prospect

## Template de prompt

```
Create a single-scene video for funnel step [NUMBER]: "[STEP NAME]".

Duration: [DURATION] seconds at 30fps.
Brand: Marin (accent #49C5B6, dark #015048, bg #FAFAFA, text #000000).
Font: Inter. Style: Clean motion graphics.

On-screen text: "[MAIN HEADLINE]"
Sub-text: "[SUB_TEXT]"

[DESCRIBE THE VISUAL — cut-out, icon, chart, or animation needed]

Animation: [DESCRIBE ENTRANCE AND MOTION]

CTA at end (last 3 seconds): "[CTA_TEXT]" with a subtle pulse.
```

## Exemple — Étape "Qualification SIRET"

```
Create a single-scene video for funnel step 3: "SIRET Qualification".

Duration: 20 seconds at 30fps.
Brand: Marin (accent #49C5B6, dark #015048, bg #FAFAFA, text #000000).
Font: Inter. Style: Clean motion graphics.

On-screen text: "We verify your business in seconds."
Sub-text: "SIRET check — automatic, no paperwork."

A document icon with a magnifying glass appears on the left.
A checkmark pulses on the right when verification passes.
Progress bar fills from 0% to 100% in the center.

Animation: Document slides in from left. Magnifying glass orbits
around it. Progress bar fills with a smooth spring. Checkmark
springs in at 100%.

CTA at end (last 3 seconds): "Continue your setup" with pulse.
```
